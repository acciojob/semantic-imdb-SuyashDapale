Raja Shivaji

The journey of the young Shivaji, who fought against the powers that be and was crowned as Chhatrapati monarch.

Summary

Shahaji Raje Bhosale is granted the land of Pune but is caught between the web of Nizamshahi and Adilshahi.He is forced to leave Pune along with his empire and shift to Bangalore.After which Moghuls act in the manner of cruelty towards poor villagers their livelihood is disrupted along with slaughter of human lives.During this period Shivaji Maharaj the savior of Maratha empire is born with dream of Swarajya.Few years later Shahahji Raje is forced to leave Bangalore but he decides to stay back and fight with elder son Sambaji and sends Shivaji Raje to Pune along with mother Jijabai.A kid Shivaji Raje notices sufferings of his villagers and decides to fight for them to make lives better.In coming years Shivaji Maharaj and Sambhaji Maharaj rebel against the Moghuls and capture their forts and attain Swarajya.The Moghul empire sees this as a threat to them and appoints a ruthless Afzal Khan to capture Shivaji Maharaj.
